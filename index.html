Intro

Paul is a seasoned AI expert and strategic consultant, specializing in helping organizations leverage artificial intelligence and data science to drive innovation. With extensive experience in large language models, cloud-based software engineering, and AI strategy, he has guided projects across multiple industries and company scales.

Paul has led numerous initiatives ranging from computer vision and AI research to scaling machine learning applications for enterprise use. He excels at integrating advanced AI technologies with practical software development in cloud environments. His project portfolio includes autonomous drones, interactive AI assistants, and autonomous language agents.

Previously, Paul served as the CEO and Head of AI at Tensora GmbH, further refining his leadership skills. By combining deep technical expertise with business acumen, he enables organizations to effectively implement AI strategies and achieve transformative results.
